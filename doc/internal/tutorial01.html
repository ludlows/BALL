Building a peptide from a given sequence

Welcome to the BALLView tutorial. It will show you some of BALLViews main features. The demo is divided in several steps, wich have to be finished one by one. The button "Next" will be enabled after finishing each individual step.

We start by building a polypeptide from an aminoacid sequence. To do so, open the PeptideBuilder dialog by clicking the menu entry
"Build->BuildPeptide".

Then enter the sequence ARND in the label field at the top. This will create a polypeptide with the aminosequence Alanin->Arginin->Asparagine->Aspartic acid.
Then press the "Build" button.
